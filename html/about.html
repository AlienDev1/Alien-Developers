Who is Jibri Tolen

I am currently an undergrad at Kennesaw State University, where I major in Software Engineering. My background is in software integration, maintenance, and development. I have worked in several capacities where I assisted with the implementation and integration of various applications ranging from the role of a Data Science Engineer utilizing R for computational analysis to a Software Engineer implementing common coding practices in compliance with OWASP. In addition, I have a strong foundation of network principles and protocols such as end-to-end encryption and reliability, Load balance, and WAF(Web application firewall) configuration. I am currently looking for the opportunity to assist people with technology by allowing their imagination to come to life.

How did this young man come to have so much experience?

Fredrick Douglas once said, "If there is no struggle there is no success!". I am identified as a self-starter, Insist on high standards for myself, and a student of life. I particularly say a student of life because I believe we can learn something new from anyone or anything; all we have to do is be still and listen. When I first started applying for entry-level positions I quickly learned about the paradox "we cannot hire you for this Jr. role due to a lack of experience". I refused to bound myself by the odds that were stacked against me I decided to make my own experience. I started implementing and expanding on the foundation that I received from my classes with small projects. In addition, I volunteered my services to my community family, and friends by doing pro bono work within the Atlanta area. Some of the requests that I received were outside of my comfort zone. Yet, I did not quit. I had many sleepless nights working tirelessly with one goal "Provide professional, efficient, and user-friendly work as if this is a million-dollar project". That mindset has expanded my foundation in development beyond words. I have even worked as OOP (Object Oriented Programming) tutor! God and my work ethic have landed me in opportunities that I could not have imagined ranging from working with N.S.B.E Aerospace S.I.G to working in exclusive environments such as Equifax within a NOC (Network Operations Center). I actively continue to build upon my foundation developing, deploying, and testing in my home lab to master my craft.
