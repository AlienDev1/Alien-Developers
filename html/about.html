Who is Jibri Tolen

I am currently an undergrad at Kennesaw State University, where I dual major in Software Engineering and Applied Mathmatics. My background is in software integration, security, maintenance, and development. I have worked in several capactities ranging from the role of a Data Science Engineer for computational analysis, Working as a Software Engineer closely with network engineers to defend the US from cyber attackers from overseas, to a Software Engineer developing tools for companies to manage billions of dollars all while implementing common coding practices in compliance with OWASP. I have about 8 years of of Enterprise Development experience. I have worked for various companies such as Chick-Fil-A corperate, Equifax, and I have done some contract work for NASA to name a few. In my experience I could not help but notice the development space is vastly under represented for people of color. In addition, for brick and mortor business to obtain the top skills to assist them in there business ventures there is a major gap in financial ability to get over that hurtle. I am here merely as not just a professional but as a resource to assist with bridge that gap and bringing top talent in a more affordable mannor. My goal is to assist people with accomplishing there dreams by way of technology by allowing and allowing there imagination to come to life.
